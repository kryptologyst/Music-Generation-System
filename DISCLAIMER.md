# Music Generation System - Privacy and Ethics Disclaimer

## IMPORTANT NOTICE

This project is designed for **RESEARCH AND EDUCATIONAL PURPOSES ONLY**. It is not intended for commercial use or production deployment without proper authorization and compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

## Privacy and Ethics Guidelines

### Data Privacy
- This system does not collect, store, or process personal identifiable information (PII)
- All audio data used for training and inference should be properly licensed and obtained with consent
- Generated music outputs do not contain personal information

### Ethical Use Restrictions
- **DO NOT** use this system to:
  - Generate copyrighted music without proper licensing
  - Create music that impersonates specific artists without permission
  - Generate music for commercial purposes without proper attribution
  - Use copyrighted training data without appropriate licenses

### Research Focus
- This system is designed to advance music generation research
- Generated outputs should be used responsibly and ethically
- Users are responsible for ensuring compliance with copyright and intellectual property laws

### Limitations
- Generated music may not be suitable for commercial use
- Quality and originality of generated music may vary
- The system is not designed to replace human composers or musicians

## Compliance
By using this system, you agree to:
1. Use it only for research and educational purposes
2. Respect copyright and intellectual property rights
3. Not use generated content for unauthorized commercial purposes
4. Comply with all applicable laws and regulations

For questions about ethical use or licensing, please contact the project maintainers.
